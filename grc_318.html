Digital Typography and Design

Recipe Pages - Madonna Pink Champagne Cake

This project was a part of my Digital Typography course. The assignment was to create unique recipe pages for your favorite recipe. One of my favorite things about living in San Luis Obispo is being right next to the Madonna Inn, specifically the Madonna Inn bakery. I chose to create my recipe pages on the Madonna Inn’s world-famous Pink Champagne Cake. I photographed the cake with my Nikon D3000, created the graphic using Procreate, and formatted the pages on InDesign.

Technical Paper - ​​Typography in Different Genres of Album Covers

This project was also a part of my Digital Typography course. The assignment was to redesign a preexisting blog or article. I chose to research typography in music, especially album covers, and came across this article http://blog.ocad.ca/wordpress/visd2004-fw2020-002-l/typography-in-different-genres-of-album-covers/: The pages were formatted using Indesign.
