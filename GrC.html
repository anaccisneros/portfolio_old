Bachelor of Science, Graphic Communication

About the Degree

Graphic Communication studies the art and science of effectively communicating ideas and messages visually. It covers every aspect of visual design, from ideation to execution across various mediums including advertising, marketing, websites, mobile apps, books, packaging, and beyond. Graphic Communication equips students with the skills to craft compelling visual narratives that captivate audiences across print and digital platforms.

Ux/UI Concentration

A concentration is a specialized course of study within a major degree program. In my User Experience/User Interface (UX/UI) concentration, I delved into website development, user experience, and interface design processes.
