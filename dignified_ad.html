Designing an Ad for SLO Life Magazine

See Ad in Magazine (page 44)

Following the Client's Vision

The image on the left shows the original design of a small ad. For my initial project as a graphic designer, I enhanced the ad by making senior care and services the focal point, using the largest font size to quickly convey the mission. I also included a QR code for rapid scanning. If I were to improve this project today, I would enhance the color's legibility by using a darker shade, particularly since it is an ad for senior care.

As a design student specialized in UX/UI, print, and digital media, my mission is to magnify beauty, whether it's in the digital realm, tangible products, or within every person I meet.
