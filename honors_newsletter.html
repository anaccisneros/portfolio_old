Electronic Newsleter, 1st Place

National Collegiate Honors Council (NCHC) 2022 Electronic Newsletter Award

As a member of the honors program, I joined the honors newsletter team, consisting of about 5 students each quarter, to produce Cal Poly's Honors Program Newsletter. The newsletter, released twice a year at the end of the fall and spring quarters, has been running since 2015 and has won several awards in the student-run electronic division at the National Collegiate Honors Council (NCHC). I am proud to be on the team that won the National Collegiate Honors Council (NCHC) 2022 Electronic Newsletter Award.
